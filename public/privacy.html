Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2024

At Bookmark Converter, your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your data when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our application, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Uploaded Files: We temporarily store your browser session data (JSON files) in order to process and convert it into downloadable bookmarks.
  • Automatically Collected Data: We may automatically collect certain data when you visit our site, such as your IP address, browser type, and usage information. This information helps us improve the performance and user experience of our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the purpose of providing the service. Specifically:

  • The uploaded session data (JSON files) is used to convert your browser session into a downloadable bookmarks file. These files are automatically deleted after 15 minutes or once you have downloaded the converted file.
  • Automatically collected data is used to analyze site traffic and improve our services.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Retention

  • Uploaded files are automatically deleted after 15 minutes or upon successful download of the converted bookmarks file, whichever comes first.
  • Any automatically collected data (e.g., usage data) may be retained for a longer period for the purpose of improving our services.

4. How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. This includes:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols.
  • Limited Retention: Uploaded files are purged after 15 minutes to minimize data exposure.
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to the system where data is processed.

5. Cookies

We may use cookies to improve your experience on our site. These cookies help us remember your preferences and track how our site is used. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect certain functionalities of the website.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website may include links to third-party services (e.g., social media platforms, GitHub). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties, so we encourage you to read their privacy policies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we collect about you.
  • Request that we delete any personal data we hold (except where required for legal or operational purposes).

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly to stay informed of any updates.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at privacy@webbified.dev.
